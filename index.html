How to use?

Here are some General steps you can follow to use this weather app:

1. To get weather information, enter your city name in the search bar or access popular cities from the menu bar.

2. Click the search button.

3. The website will display the current weather conditions for your selected city, including temperature, humidity, and wind speed.
